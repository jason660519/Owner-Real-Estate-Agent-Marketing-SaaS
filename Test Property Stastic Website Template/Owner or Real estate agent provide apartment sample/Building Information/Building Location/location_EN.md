# Prime Location | Southbank

## In the Heart of Melbourne's Arts & Entertainment

**Australia 108** is situated in Melbourne's vibrant **Southbank** precinct, the convergence point of the city's culture, arts, and premier entertainment. Living here, you will enjoy unparalleled convenience and a rich lifestyle.

### Walk Score: 99

- **Transport Hub**: Walking distance to Flinders Street Station, easily connecting you to all parts of Melbourne.
- **Shopper's Paradise**: Adjacent to the Melbourne CBD shopping district and Collins Street boutiques.
- **Culinary Delights**: Southbank Promenade hosts numerous world-class restaurants, riverside cafes, and bars.

### Culture & Entertainment

- **Crown Casino**: The Southern Hemisphere's largest entertainment complex, just steps away.
- **Arts Precinct**: The National Gallery of Victoria (NGV) and Arts Centre are close by, allowing you to immerse yourself in art at any time.
- **Royal Botanic Gardens**: An urban oasis, perfect for your morning run or weekend picnic.

Living at Australia 108, the very best of Melbourne is right at your feet.
