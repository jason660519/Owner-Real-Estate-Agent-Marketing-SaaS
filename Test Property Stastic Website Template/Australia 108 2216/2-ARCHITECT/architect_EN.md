# Masterpiece | Design & Architecture

## An Architectural Miracle by a World-Class Team

Australia 108 brings together the world's leading architecture and design teams to forge this epoch-making skyscraper.

### Architectural Design: Fender Katsalidis

Designed by the legendary architects **Fender Katsalidis**, the firm behind Melbourne's iconic Eureka Tower. Australia 108 showcases the highest standard of Australian architecture to the world with its unique streamlined appearance and iconic golden "Starburst" structure.

### Interior Design: Hirsch Bedner Associates (HBA)

With 50 years of experience in designing top-tier luxury hotels, **HBA** brings the refinement and comfort of six-star hotels into residential design. Every corner is meticulously crafted to elevate the quality of life for residents and reveal a true state of luxury.

### Spatial Aesthetics: Carr Design Group

As one of Australia's most influential design firms, **Carr Design Group** injects a unique soul into Australia 108's communal facilities with its modern, minimalist, and textural design language, creating social spaces that are both grand and intimate.
